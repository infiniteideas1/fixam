Service FAQ

How long does recovery take?

Each case is unique. While some recoveries are completed within 24-48 hours, complex forensic cases may take 7-14 business days depending on the platform's response depth.

Is my data safe during the process?

Yes. We use strict end-to-end encryption for all shared credentials and sensitive documents. Once a recovery is confirmed, all session tokens and temporary logs are wiped.

Do you guarantee success?

While we maintain a 98% success rate, final outcomes often depend on platform-side policies. However, our direct lines and technical methods offer the highest probability of recovery available.

What platforms do you support?

We specialized in a wide range of platform recoveries including Facebook, Instagram, Email (Gmail/Outlook), Crypto Wallets, WhatsApp, Reddit, Discord, Telegram, YouTube, X (Twitter), and Microsoft accounts.
