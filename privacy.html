Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

At Fixapathway, we prioritize the confidentiality and security of your digital identity. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our cybersecurity and account recovery services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and details regarding your compromised accounts solely for the purpose of recovery and security auditing. We do not store sensitive passwords once the service is complete.

3. Use of Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide account recovery and security services.
  • Communicate with you regarding your case.
  • Improve our security protocols and services.

4. Data Protection

We implement military-grade encryption and strict access controls to protect your data. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not share your information with third parties except as necessary to perform our services (e.g., contacting platform support on your behalf) or as required by law.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at fixapathway@gmail.com.
